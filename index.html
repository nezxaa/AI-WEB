About Adel

Reva Fidela Adel Pantjoro, better known as Adel, is a former member of JKT48's Team T. She joined JKT48 as part of the 8th generation in April 2019 and was promoted to Team T in 2020. Known for her sweet voice and charming personality, Adel quickly became a fan favorite.

Adel graduated from JKT48 on June 6, 2024, with her final stage performance being "Aturan Anti Cinta". She continues to be active in entertainment and social media, sharing her love for music, gaming, and connecting with fans.
